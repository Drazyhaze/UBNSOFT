OpenSource CoE

UBNSoft Solutions has gained immense expertise in Open Source development. The experience has come by working with several clients across domains. Globally, IT requirements from various organizations are adopting open source technology as it eliminates exuberant licensing costs, vendor lock-ins and product dependencies. Over the years, open source has witnessed vast improvements and has gained significant ground. It is a technology that is universally accessible, developed and supported in a collaborative manner by a strong network of independent developers. Open source technologies improve IT application adaptability and flexibility. This helps to massively reduce operational costs.

UBNSoft Solution's experience in Open Source software has resulted in delivering some of the most efficient, stable and widely used software products ever. Today, Open Source initiatives are leaping ahead in terms of generating customer satisfaction as compared to any proprietary solution frameworks or development platforms. The output is equally competitive. Our clients prefer products working with Open Source software, as it enables project to be done better, faster and cheaper. This technology is high on performance and availability that enables developers to deliver faster and high-quality solutions.

Solution Benefits from UBN:
  • Well defined strategies and roadmap in line with your organization requirements.
  • Successful integration of open source technology without any hassles.
  • Effective interoperable solutions result in improves ROI and delivery efficiency.
  • Leverage open source technologies to streamline IT application portfolio.
  • Completely eliminate vendor lock-ins and product dependencies.
